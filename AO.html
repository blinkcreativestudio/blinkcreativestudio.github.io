ABOUT THE POSITION

Are you a dynamic individual with a passion for organizational efficiency and a keen eye for detail? Look no further! We’re on the lookout for talented professionals to join our team as an Administrative Officer!

Job Summary:

As an Admin/HR Officer your responsibilities would encompass managing administrative tasks, coordinating HR processes such as recruitment, onboarding for diverse roles in the multimedia industry, payroll, compensation and benefits, punctuality, tardiness, and opening of accounts at Union Bank. You'd handle employee records, facilitate communication between departments, and ensure compliance with HR policies. Additionally, your role might involve supporting or supervising multimedia projects and studio activities such as photoshoots and client inquiries.

Qualifications and Skills:
