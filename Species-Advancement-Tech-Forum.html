The first  Species Advancement Tech Forum (SATF) is set to become the premier platform for pioneering the future of livestock through science, technology, and strategic collaboration. SATF brings together leading minds in genetics, nutrition, housing, and animal health to drive species development into the next generation. Expect powerful keynote talks, cutting-edge science, and immersive networking, designed for industry leaders shaping what comes next.

Whether you're mapping genomes, optimizing feed, or building the next smart farm-this is where advancement begins!
